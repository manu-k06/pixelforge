ABOUT PIXELFORGE

Curated by AI. Built for your screen.

Built Different

PixelForge is an AI-curated platform that automatically discovers and filters the finest wallpapers from across the internet.

Every image runs through a rigorous AI pipeline judging quality, composition, and aesthetic appeal. We are currently serving curated wallpapers across 9 categories, updated automatically via our python extraction tasks.

How It Works

Step 1

Discover

Our autonomous python scripts tirelessly scan thousands of images daily from the most prominent design subreddits across the entire internet.

Step 2

Filter

Our integrated AI heuristics forcefully judge every scraped image for exact lighting, resolution ratio compliance, and overwhelming aesthetic impact.

Step 3

Curate

Only the absolute finest, distraction-free visual wallpapers survive the pipeline and make it to the permanent live PixelForge library.
